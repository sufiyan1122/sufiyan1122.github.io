As an imaginative and pasionate photographer with 7+ year tract of successful photography, be it nature or candid here i am... introducing myself as Haresh patil and my dream venture crafted by mahself and thus named as 'Dream Crafter'.
Not much to say about me but people know me as Harry and here's my quick story so far. A little over nine years ago in 2008, i was gifted a camera by my big bro when i was pursuing my 3rd year Diploma in Commercial Arts from L. S. Raheja school of Arts, Mumbai. And there was this photographer born in me came out clicking moments. i have this habbit of capturing moments and convert them into beautiful memories. it startrd since then and the love of cliclking increased day by day. Very soon i realised i have found something which i always wanted to do.. May be yes.. I was born to click! Though i was trained to be a Commercial Artist. i have 7+ year of experience in Advertising too but what i do now is tell stories. Even though there was ups and downs in my life till date, i never leave my cameras aside. Being fond of roaming, Bike riding.. known as decent rider and trekking. My hobby of photography then took a turn towards passion. Today i have almost all my collection in store for where ever i have been. The collection includes the glimpses of Leh Ladakh to Goa beaches or may it be any single weekend trek. My only intention was to take better photos. I quickly realized how much i loved having a camera in my hands.

My passion get recorgnition and hence could display my photos in many reputed exhibitions and that's when i sought of got a tag of being a photographer. Owing to my passion, I work continuously to learn new thigs in order to make my work professional. Dreams do come true if u really go for it!
